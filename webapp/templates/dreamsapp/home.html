Introduction

Sleep musicalization is a novel way to aid the analysis and understanding of sleep measurement data.

automatic music composition algorithm is used to generate a song from the sleep data.

Sleep musicalization produces music that complements visualizations of sleep measurements.

The musicalization project has been carried out in the Discovery Research Group at the University of Helsinki.

Sleep analysis is based on the Beddit sleep measurement sensor. Sleep measurements and analysis results are retrieved from the Beddit service, by the user's authorization.
